Tips for Effective Reporting

  • Take clear photos: Include multiple angles and ensure the issue is well-lit and visible.
  • Be specific with location: Use precise addresses or drop a pin on the map for accurate GPS coordinates.
  • Provide detailed descriptions: Include size, severity, and any safety concerns related to the issue.
  • Enable notifications: Get updates when your reported issues are being addressed or resolved.
